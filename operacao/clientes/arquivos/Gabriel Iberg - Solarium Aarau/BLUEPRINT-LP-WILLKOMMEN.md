## Seção 3 — THE PROBLEM  *(empatia, "pain is the pitch")*

**Objetivo:** nomear a dor real em poucas linhas para o tráfego frio se reconhecer.

**Título:** Getting a tan shouldn't be complicated.
**Copy:**
> Grey Swiss winter and pale skin. Studios that make you book, wait, or sign up. Places that still
> want cash or a prepaid card before you can even start. And the quiet worry on your first visit:
> what if I don't know how it works?
>
> Solarium Aarau removes all of that.

## Seção 4 — BENEFITS + COLLARIUM  *(a solução e o diferencial)*

**Objetivo:** por que vale a pena, e por que o Collarium é melhor que um solário comum.
Ataca a dor #6 (medo de resultado artificial).

**Título:** A natural tan, powered by Collarium.
**Intro:** Collarium combines UV and red light in one session. You get a tan and skincare at the
same time. This is how modern tanning works.

**Benefit cards (4):**
1. **Even, natural tan** — Up to 50% more direct pigmentation than standard tanning. No orange, just
   a natural, even glow.
2. **Kinder to your skin** — Red light helps skin feel softer and smoother while you tan.
3. **Your daily dose of light** — A boost of Vitamin D and wellbeing, especially in the darker
   months.
4. **Modern, well-kept machines** — Top Ergoline / Collarium equipment, clean and maintained.

## Seção 5 — HOW IT WORKS  *(3 passos CASHLESS — corrige o erro da v1)*

**Objetivo:** provar que é fácil e rápido. Fluxo cashless correto. Mata a dor #4 (receio/fricção).

**Título:** Your tan in 3 simple steps.
**Steps:**
1. **Choose your cabin** — Pick the cabin you want, no booking, no waiting.
2. **Pay at the machine** — Set your time and pay with card or TWINT right there. No studio card,
   no account, no top-up.
3. **Step in and relax** — Start your session and enjoy.

**Nota de reforço (abaixo dos passos):** A video guide runs on the screen above every machine, so
you always know what to do, even on your very first visit.

## Seção 9 — FAQ  *(objeções, accordion reutilizado)*

**Objetivo:** derrubar objeções finais. Adaptado ao contexto cashless.

- **Do I need to book?** No. Just walk in. We're open every day from 06:00 to 24:00.
- **Do I need a studio card?** No. With card or TWINT you pay directly at the machine. A studio card
  is only needed if you want to pay with cash.
- **It's my first time. Is it hard to use?** Not at all. A video guide runs on the screen above
  every machine and shows you exactly what to do.
- **How do I pay?** Card, debit or TWINT right at the machine. Cash is also possible via a studio card.
- **Is it clean?** Yes. Cabins are cleaned and maintained, and the team is around to keep everything
  in order. See our Google reviews.
